Description
West Santo Travel sends non-marketing SMS and MMS messages to travelers and volunteer drivers for trip operations. Messages are triggered after a recipient opts in through our public SMS opt-in page at https://wst.prabandha.dpdns.org/opt-in. Messages include flight reminders, schedule change notices, airport pickup and drop-off coordination, driver assignment updates, and related operational travel alerts. Message frequency varies based on active travel plans. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.

How do end-users consent to receive messages?
End users opt in through our public SMS opt-in page at https://wst.prabandha.dpdns.org/opt-in. On that page, the user enters their full name, mobile phone number, and whether they are enrolling as a passenger or driver, reviews the SMS/MMS disclosure, and must check an unchecked consent checkbox before submitting. The disclosure states: "I agree to receive operational SMS/MMS messages from West Santo Travel about travel reminders, schedule changes, and transport coordination. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help." The page links to our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions. After opt-in, the user receives an immediate confirmation message: "West Santo Travel: You are subscribed to operational travel text updates. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel." These messages are transactional and operational only, not marketing.
